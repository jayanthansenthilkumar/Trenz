Built a Resume - Professional CV Competition

March 18, 2025
1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Individual Participation

Competition Details

  • Bring your laptop with Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or any preferred editor.
  • Participants will have 3 hours to build a professional resume from scratch.
  • A basic template will be provided, but creativity and customization are encouraged.
  • Your resume will be judged on design, organization, content quality, and originality.
  • All information in your resume must be factual - no fictitious details allowed.
  • Include your education, skills, projects, and achievements in your resume.
  • Participants may use online resources but no pre-made templates are permitted.
  • Final submissions must be in PDF format.
  • Prizes will be awarded for "Best Overall Resume," "Most Creative Design," and "Most Professional."
  • Top resumes will be featured in our college placement guide.
  • Refreshments will be provided during the event.
