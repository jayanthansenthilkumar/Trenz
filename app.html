App Dev Pro Challenge

App Dev Pro Challenge 2025

March 15, 2025
10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Individual/Team (max 2 members)

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participants can compete individually or in teams of maximum 2 members.
  • The app theme/problem statement will be announced at the start of the competition.
  • Participants must create a functional mobile application with an intuitive user interface.
  • Apps will be judged on UI/UX design, functionality, innovation, performance, and adherence to the theme.
  • Participants can use any mobile development framework (Flutter, React Native, Kotlin, Swift, etc.).
  • Participants must bring their own laptops with necessary development environments pre-installed.
  • Internet access will be provided for reference and to download necessary packages.
  • Basic app functionality must be demonstrated even if the entire app is not complete.
  • Participants must submit their work 15 minutes before the end of the competition.
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.
