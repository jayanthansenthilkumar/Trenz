Code Quest - Coding Treasure Hunt Challenge

March 17, 2025
10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Teams of 2-3 members

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams must consist of 2-3 members from the same institution.
  • The treasure hunt will have multiple checkpoints, each with a unique coding challenge.
  • To proceed to the next checkpoint, teams must solve the current challenge correctly.
  • Challenges may involve algorithms, data structures, cryptography, or logical puzzles.
  • Languages supported: Python, Java, JavaScript, and C++.
  • Each team will receive a digital map with initial clues at the start of the event.
  • Teams can use their own laptops and development environments.
  • Internet access will be limited to official documentation websites only.
  • Teams found sharing solutions with others will be disqualified.
  • Winners will be determined based on completion time and accuracy of solutions.
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.
